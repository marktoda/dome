These reviewer notes are out of scope for this branch:

- **Agent-readiness #4 ‚Äî shipped-config axiom invariants map:** A new contributor adding the 16th invariant might wonder if it needs an entry in `SHIPPED_VAULT_CONFIG.invariants`.
